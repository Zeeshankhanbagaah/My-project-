Why LanDollar?

How LanDollar Works

The revolutionary process that transforms real estate into stable digital currency

1

Mortgage Origination

Property owner applies for a hybrid mortgage through a participating lender. Property undergoes appraisal and due diligence.

2

Minting LanDollar

Up to 50% of property value is minted as LanDollar tokens to fund the senior debt portion of the mortgage.

3

Hybrid Mortgage Creation

Combination of LanDollar (senior debt) and USD (junior debt) creates a secure two-tiered mortgage structure secured by the property.

4

Principal Repayment

As principal is repaid in LanDollar, tokens are burned, reducing supply. Junior debt is repaid in USD with interest.
